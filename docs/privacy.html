Privacy Policy

Echoes of Vasteria is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

Data Collection

Echoes of Vasteria does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We believe in keeping your gaming experience private and secure.

No Analytics or Tracking

The game contains no advertisements, analytics, or tracking mechanisms. Your gameplay data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Local Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is only accessible to you and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the game through Steam, please refer to Steam's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Steam community page for Echoes of Vasteria.
